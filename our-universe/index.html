Overview of our products

Since 1981, AutoUSA is sourcing, importinng and exporting thousands of products from many different sectors according to the request of its customers. Here some unusual examples of past realizations: official supplier for South America of 1998 World Cup’s items, supplier of high-end alimentary goods for the “Relais et Chateaux”, logistician and provider for all dinner’s reception of 1997 French presidential visit to South America, creation of several distribution network in South America, signing of the first contracts of tennis players under Babolat brand, implementation of top-end channels for tableware products as a representative for brands like Asa Selection or Sia, set up of partnership in Brasil and Paraguay for Eugene Perma hair products, without forgetting the field that made us recognized: sports with windsurf, diving, nautical world, skiing, fishing and camping.

The universe of AutoUSA doesn’t have any limits, we propose you here a non-exhaustive glimpse of products and brands we offer, classified by categories.
